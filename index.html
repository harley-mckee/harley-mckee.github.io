Hi, I'm Harley. Welcome to my site!


Here I upload my latest projects and thinking. Look around and you should understand more about my personality and perspective. I hope that something here connects with you and/or challenges your thinking in some way.

Health challenges this past year limited my ability to follow creative dreams. These days I feel more capable - physically and mentally. A little pain and struggle helped me to put some priorities in check. I decided one priority is to create more. So here I am.

Quick facts about me:

  • I come from the Bay Area, live in Los Angeles and move soon to New York City
  • I love learning about how the mind works
  • I pride myself in my ability to listen
  • I read, exercise and sit quietly each day to keep sane
